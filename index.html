My Journey

Hi!! Thanks for stopping by!

I am a former biotechnologist, turned UX designer. After years of working as a biotechnologist, I found myself with an overwhelmed desire to engage myself creatively in something more productive. Since my childhood, I have always been drawn towards arts and enjoyed being surrounded by soothing music and my paintings, including the failed ones as well.


UX design appealed to me as it embodies many elements of my scientific profession, including user-centric products, research analysis, and attention to detail. In addition, I am passionate about improving lives through design and constantly look forward to learning and growing while empathizing with people and crafting meaningful experiences.

I consider design as an art, and if something has to last, it has to be art. A work that combines visual design with usability is well worth the undertaking.


When not working, one can find me with my family or spending time with our favorite furry companion, Coco - a spoiled lab or indulging in my hobbies.! I have a website on "hobbies" that I created, it is “https://hobbies.community/”. So please take a look, and if you are a hobbyist like me, don't forget to give me a virtual high-five!! I love being close to the lush nature and wavy waterfronts, and ohh! not to mention my love for food.
